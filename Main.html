About Us

FloatChat is an AI-powered conversational interface that allows researchers, policymakers, and communities to query, explore, and visualize ARGO float ocean data in plain English. By combining Large Language Models (LLMs), vector databases, and geospatial dashboards, FloatChat democratizes access to complex oceanographic datasets.

💡 Our Innovations

🧠 Conversational AI

Transforms natural language into SQL queries, making ocean data accessible without coding.

🌍 Dynamic Visualizations

Interactive maps, trajectories, and depth-time plots bring ARGO data to life in real time.

🗣️ Inclusivity Layer

Multi-language support and voice-enabled features ensure accessibility for all communities.

⚠️ Proactive Disaster Alerts

Instant recognition of cyclone, tsunami, and flooding risks with urgent safety notifications.

🎮 Gamified Learning

AI tutor mode and interactive learning make ocean science engaging for students worldwide.
